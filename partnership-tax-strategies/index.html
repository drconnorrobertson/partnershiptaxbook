About This Book

Partnerships are one of the most flexible and powerful business structures in the tax code -- but that flexibility comes with complexity. Most business owners and investors know they have a partnership or an LLC taxed as a partnership, but few truly understand how partnership taxation works, how it affects their bottom line, or how to use it to their advantage.

Partnership Tax Strategies was written to change that. Drawing on years of hands-on experience advising business owners and real estate investors across the country, AE Tax Advisors breaks down every major aspect of partnership taxation into clear, actionable chapters. From the fundamentals of pass-through taxation to advanced topics like special allocations, Section 754 elections, and self-employment tax planning, every chapter is grounded in real-world scenarios.

This is not a textbook. It is a practical guide built for people who want to understand the tax implications of their partnership -- and use that understanding to build wealth. Whether you receive a K-1 every year and have never fully understood it, or you are forming a new partnership and want to get the tax structure right from day one, this book is for you.
