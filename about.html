About AE Tax Advisors

Making sophisticated tax knowledge accessible to the business owners, investors, and entrepreneurs who need it most.

AE Tax Advisors is a national tax advisory firm headquartered in Billings, Montana. The firm works with business owners, real estate investors, and entrepreneurs across the country -- helping them reduce their tax burden through proactive planning, strategic structuring, and deep expertise in the tax code.

The firm's core specialties include cost segregation studies, short-term rental tax strategy, partnership taxation, entity structuring, and comprehensive real estate tax planning. AE Tax Advisors is not a compliance-only firm -- the focus is on advisory, strategy, and implementation.

Why We Publish

Tax strategy should not be locked behind closed doors. The strategies in our books are the same ones we implement for our clients every day. We believe that when business owners understand the tax code, they make better decisions -- for their businesses, their partnerships, and their families.

Our books are written for clarity, not complexity. Every concept is explained in plain language with real-world examples. We do not write academic textbooks -- we write practical guides that you can read on a weekend and start implementing on Monday.
